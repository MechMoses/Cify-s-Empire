About Us

Located in the heart of Tumfure Market, Gombe, Cify’s Empire is your go-to fashion destination for stunning native styles, bridal elegance, and ready-to-wear pieces. We blend culture and creativity into every design, leaving a lasting impression.

Our Services

  • Women’s Traditional Wear Timeless and elegant designs tailored to showcase African heritage with a modern twist.
  • Bridal Styling & Accessories From gowns to finishing touches, we make your big day unforgettable.
  • Custom Tailoring Personalized fittings to bring your dream outfit to life with precision and perfection.
  • Ready-to-Wear Outfits Chic and classy pieces available instantly for every occasion.
  • Men’s Native Designs Stylish agbadas, kaftans, and traditional wears crafted for confidence.
